Gamemodes


Ever tired of the same old Survival, Creative, and Adventure mode? Here's some new flavors to your Minecraft to spice things up.
Note: These gamemodes require Host Privliges, which will disable throphies, and most require at least 2 players.
(You can change the rules if you want)

Gladiator

Mode: Multiple, Difficulty: Any, Minimum Players: 2

Disable PvP, daylight and weather cycle, and start in Creative Mode. Build an area to duel in, now enable the daylight and weather cycle and swap all players to Survival mode. Players will spend a couple days (however many you decide) preparing for battle by gaining materials, once time is up, enable PvP and duke it out in the Arena.

You are not allowed to

  • Have monsters, golems, or animals fight for you
  • Use TNT or End Crystals (they ruin the map)


Eternal Darkness

Mode: Survival, Difficulty: Hard, Minimum Players: 1

Set time to night, wait about 5 minutes, and Disable daylight cycle. Thats all.

You are not allowed to

  • Disable mob spawning


Spirit Mode

Mode: Survival, Difficulty: Hard, Minimum Players: 2

Each player has a number of lives, when lives run out, you turn invisible in Creative mode, and kill all players without damaging them directly.

You are not allowed to

  • Hide from danger, Actively seek it
  • Share items, houses, or anything, trading is allowed
  • Kill defenseless players. It wouldn't be fun if everyone lost thier lives immedietly


On the other side of the spectrum, Minecraft may be too exciting for you, so bore yourself with this one

Camels back

Mode: Survival, Difficulty: Peaceful, Minimum Players: 1

One life, no natural regeneration.

You are not allowed to

  • Gain health by any means
  • Equip armor
