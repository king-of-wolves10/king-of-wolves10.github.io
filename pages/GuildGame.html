Plot

You're a Lizard in a test lab, trapped in a glass container. A scientist injects the player with something unknown. This causes the player to feel ill and start shaking. Suddenly, glass shatters, and the table below you crashes to the ground. The sceintist flees for cover, to avoid the glass. Once he uncovers, he sees that the player has turned to a velociraptor, but before he could celebrate, the player runs through the wall and over yonder before he has the chance to catch you. He is the main antagonist.

Your motive

You wish to be free of all experiments, and find yourself someplace home.

His motive

He wishes to control you, and profit out of his discovery

Gameplay

The game is a 2D Platformer. You must reach a goal at the end of the level to win. The main gimmick is the powerup system, there will be three kinds of powerups. Strength, Speed, Jump. Collecting a combination of these will morph you into different things. The more you collect, the stronger you are. Getting hit as will make you lose a heart, when all hearts are lost, you'll lose your form, or worse, die if you have no form (lizard).
